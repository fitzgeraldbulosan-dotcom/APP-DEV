#  *Avengers: Endgame*

**Avengers: Endgame** is one of the most successful superhero films ever made.
It was produced by **Marvel Studios** and directed by *Anthony and Joe Russo*.
The movie shows the **final battle** of the Avengers against *Thanos* to restore balance in the universe.

---

## **Summary**

After Thanos wiped out half of all life in the universe, the remaining Avengers work together to **reverse the snap**.
They travel through time to collect the Infinity Stones and bring everyone back.
The film ends with a huge final battle where *Iron Man* sacrifices himself to save humanity.

> “Part of the journey is the end.” — *Tony Stark*

---

### **Main Characters**

1. **Iron Man (Tony Stark)** – The genius inventor who made the ultimate sacrifice.
2. **Captain America (Steve Rogers)** – The brave leader who chose peace after the war.
3. **Thor** – The god of thunder who finds strength again after loss.
4. **Black Widow (Natasha Romanoff)** – The spy who gives her life for the mission.
5. **Hulk (Bruce Banner)** – Combines his intelligence and strength as *Smart Hulk*.
